How to Register

Registration instructuons:

  1. Follow the link to the tetonscouts.org website.
  2. Under program info, click the link that says “Week #2: July 31-August 5, 2023”.
  3. Scroll all the way to the bottom and click the button that says “Register”.
  4. Create a New User profile, or Log On.
  5. Select how many participants you are signing up, and type in their names.
  6. Fill out the participant form. Sign up for Venturing with Crew Anthony with Amory Crook. Unit Number: 1067, or if you are already registered with a different unit, you can put that number down.
  7. Fill Out Billing Information and Pay
  8. All done! Keep the receipt, and if you have any questions, call or text Amory at (307) 226-0217.
